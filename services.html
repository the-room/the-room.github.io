  Beauty

 

Waxing - using a combination of Wax:One and The Sugar Company products to provide the most comfortable waxing experience.

Brow Lamination - involves the “perming” of your eyebrow hairs as it gives them a set, uniform shape which gives them a fuller more even look for an extended period of time. Brow lamination can be used to enhance your natural eyebrows giving them a more defined shape, and can help create a fuller look where there may be sparseness to the brow area. Brow lamination can be paired with or without a brow tint depending on the desired end look, and this is why is works for so many people , the shape and boldness of the eyebrows can be adapted for every individual person. Brow lamination lasts between 6-8 weeks with minimal maintenance required. We use the well known brand 'London Brow' who's products are vegan and cruelty free to carryout our brow lamination treatments. A patch test is required 48 hours prior to treatment.

Eyelash and Eyebrow Tint - this is a treatment where specific semi permanent dyes specifically designed for these areas are used in a range of shades to give a more defined appearance, without the need of mascara and eyebrow make up. On average the tint in the lashes will last 6-8 weeks and on the eyebrows it will last as a stain on the skin for up to 1 week but will remain on the eyebrow hairs for 2-4 weeks. A patch test is required 48 hours prior to treatment.

Eyelash Lift and Tint - the Eyelash lift treatment works with the client’s natural lashes to create the appearance of longer, thicker and more visible lashes. By straightening the natural lashes at the root, the lift instantly gives extra volume, definition and length. to complete the eyelash lift a deep intense tint is used to both the upper and lower lashes for that extra wow factor. To create this look we use the range of LASHUS products. A patch test is also required for 48 hours prior to treatment.

Henna Brows - Henna is a semi-permanent treatment with dye, which Unlike traditional eyebrow dye, eyebrow henna not only dyes the eyebrow hairs but also the skin. The purpose of the treatment is to cast a shadow or create an illusion of depth and fullness in the brow area, visually filling in any sparseness.this creates a tattoo effect, so you no longer need to use makeup to redraw your eyebrows. Brow Henna delivers long-lasting colour on the skin for up to 2 weeks and the eyebrow hairs for up to 6 weeks. Since the skin is also treated with color, you get very beautiful, expressive results from henna eyebrows. A big plus of henna brows is the option of customization: You can choose which color works best for your client’s skin tone and brow hairs. Henna allows you to create the perfect blend by mixing and matching different color combinations. Even stunning results with Ombré-brows are possible if that is the preferred option. The brand of henna used is supercillium which consists mainly of natural ingredients which is great for using with sensitive skin types. A patch test is required 48 hours prior to treatment.

Facials - using Neal's Yard organic and naturalproducts to give skin specific facial treatments.

Full facial treatments includes cleanse, tone, exfoliation, mask, massage, finished with a range of serums and creams. The benefit of regular facials is to release tension and stress, which helps reduce the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles. Regular facials and lymphatic drainage reducing inflammation, puffiness, congestion and fluid build up of facids, also stimulating the blood circulation helping to lift and tone the skin and boost the production of colagen, skin healing and re-juvenation which gives the skin a healthier, smoother, clearer appearance.

Dermaplaning - is a non-invasive resurfacing exfoliation treatment using a surgical blade to remove dead skin cells and vellous hair.

Dermaplaning removes up to 21 day old dead skin cells which helps even out skin tone which minimisises the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles, giving a brighter, more radiant , smoother clearer complexion. Dermaplaning will also aid the deeper penetration of products into the skin providing the best results possible of products used. This treatment is recommended every 4-6 weeks.

Diamond Microdermabrasion - is a non-surgical skin procedure by using diamond heads to abrade or rub off the top skin layer which also vacuums up the dead skin particles at the same time.

The stainless still surounds are embedded with 3 different grades 120grit, 80 grit and 50 grit which come in various sizes and are attached to stainless steel wands to provide the ultimate exfoliating treatment. Microdermabrasion is an extremely effective treatment that improves the appearance and function of the skin. Its known to be effective against aging skin, pigmentation, blackheads and fine line and wrinkles through the regeneration of the epidermal cell structure. This treatment is recommended as a initial course of six every one to two weeks to achieve maximum results.

FaceLite Red Led Mask Treatment - is an anti-ageing red light and near infra-red phototherapy treatment which is used to stimulate collagen and elastin production and stimulate the blood flow to the skin to improve the appearance and function of the skin.

It also helps to reduce pores, hydrate the skin and reduce the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles which helps the skin to look rejuvenated and plumper. This treatment can be used alongside and facial treatments to help enhance the benefits if those treatments, but it can also be used independently as a frequent treatment.

Body Treatments - massage has immense value. To obtain the maximium benefits and to personalise the massage the most suitable oils will be chosen from either the Neal's Yard pre-blended or carrier oils with added aromatherapy oils

Massage is the manipulation of the bodys soft tissue, which is generally for the treatment of body stress or pain. Massage has immense value to the body and mind by reducing stress, increasing relaxation, reducing pain, muscle soreness and tension. It improves circulation and energy, it lowers the heart rate and blood pressure, and improves immune function. To maximise the benefits of massage a consultation will take place before the treatment to establish any specific areas of discomfort and the massage will be carried out using a blend of Neals Yard organic pre-blended massage oils.

Hands and Feet - using a both Neals Yard and CND shellac products to create the ultimate manicure experience for your hands which gives long lasting results of up to three weeks when using the CND shellac Gel polish.

For feet treatments Elim products are used, which are a medical grade pedicure treatment that uses state-of-the-art ingredients to remove hard skin and calluses from your feet to give soft smooth results that last.
