  About

 

the room is a small business within Gloucestershire that provides hair and beauty treatments.The business name; the room originates from a room created for the business that allows for a beauty salon experience when using the room's services.

the room is run and owned by Alison, who provides a wide range of services including hair cutting, colouring, styling through to waxing and shellac nails. The business is based within Alison's family home and uses a custom designed and built room for the sole purpose of providing hair and beauty services.

Alison is a qualified NVQ level 3 beauty therapist and a NVQ level 2 hairdresser, completing her core training in 2006 at Shrewsbury college. Since qualifying Alison has gained various professional qualifications and attended numerous seminars/courses which have added to her skillset.
